### Kachra Seth — urban waste management platform

`React` `Vite` `FastAPI` `MongoDB` `Python` — 3rd place, Anveshana 2025

This is the project I'd point you to if you want to see what I can build. It's a waste management platform with a citizen-facing app and a dashboard for municipal staff.

- Bins get tracked with QR codes instead of IoT sensors, cutting deployment costs by around 90%
- Collection routes are optimized with a graph traversal algorithm, bringing fuel costs down 30% and improving collection efficiency by 40%
- There's an AI piece that looks at a photo of trash and tells you which bin it belongs in
- The whole backend is REST APIs I built myself, including a Green Score leaderboard actual city stakeholders use
